Our Story

Albulena Kafexhiu

Albulena Cakes was founded in September 2020 by Albulena Kafexhiu. Albulena started by baking cakes for children's birthday parties before realizing how popular cake was with adults too!

After one year, selling more than 800 cakes, the business continues to grow from strength to strength, with our online store in Rahovec, Kosovo. As this is still a family business we have tried to keep all the ingredients seasonal, well sourced and of the highest quality. Everything is hand iced and baked in small batches to really give the essence of simple old fashioned cooking.

When we send a cake, we know we aren’t just sending dessert. we are sending a moment of celebration, joy, healing and togetherness. We Take The Cake makes it easy to delight your family, friends and colleagues with our freshly baked desserts delivered right to their doorstep ready to enjoy. Our experienced bakers mix all of our batters and frostings by hand, just like grandma used to make. We always bake with whole buttermilk, fresh eggs and real butter – never a cake mix or commercially made frostings.
